Privacy Policy for Priorities

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Priorities is a local-first productivity application designed to respect user privacy.

Data Collection

Priorities does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

All tasks, habits, settings, and usage data remain stored locally on the user's device.

Priorities does not:

  • require account creation
  • use third-party analytics
  • track user activity
  • collect location data
  • collect advertising identifiers
  • sell user data

Third-Party Services

Priorities may use Apple system services necessary for app functionality, including:

  • notifications
  • widgets
  • iCloud (if enabled in future versions)

These services are governed by Apple's privacy policies.
